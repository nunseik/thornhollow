## Common Pitfalls to Avoid

### Don't:
- ‚ùå Make characters perfectly trusting immediately
- ‚ùå Have witch be purely evil with no motivation
- ‚ùå Gloss over trauma and consequences
- ‚ùå Make victory too clean or easy
- ‚ùå Forget about introduced plot elements
- ‚ùå Skip emotional payoffs (especially rescue)
- ‚ùå Have epilogue be perfectly happy with no struggles

### Do:
- ‚úÖ Build trust gradually through shared danger
- ‚úÖ Give antagonist understandable (if not justifiable) motivation
- ‚úÖ Show psychological and physical costs
- ‚úÖ Make victory bittersweet and earned
- ‚úÖ Resolve or remove all Chekhov's guns
- ‚úÖ Give emotional scenes proper space
- ‚úÖ Show ongoing recovery and complexity
