## Characters

**Protagonists:**
- **Theron Ashborne** - Clever wizard, protagonist
- **Whisper** - Ancient rat familiar, witty companion
- **Sera** - Warrior searching for her daughter
- **Tomren** - Loyal soldier, Sera's companion

**Antagonist:**
- **The Witch of Thornhollow** - Powerful, ancient, corrupted

**Supporting:**
- **Elina** - Sera's daughter, captive
- **Maren** - Apothecary with personal vendetta

## Story Arc

1. **Prologue-Chapter 1:** Theron escapes prison with Whisper's help
2. **Chapters 2-6:** Team up with Sera/Tomren, plan assault on castle
3. **Chapters 7-11:** Infiltrate castle, battle witch, rescue Elina
4. **Epilogue:** Establish Thornhollow Institute three months later
