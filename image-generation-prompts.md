## Global Style Guidelines

**Base Style and Technique:** Semi-realistic fantasy illustration with cinematic grounded realism and a professional concept art aesthetic. Hand-painted digital illustration combined with refined variable-weight ink outlines and weighted contour lines defining silhouette and facial structure. Delicate fine-line hatching used for shadows, form, and surface texture. Forms constructed through a controlled blend of soft painterly brushwork and sharp, inked linework. Clearly illustrated, hand-drawn concept art aesthetic; visible inked contours and surface texture. Not a photorealistic 3D render; no smooth CGI shading or physically based rendering. ArtStation-quality finish.

**Global Negative Prompt:** photorealistic render, CGI, 3D model look, smooth plastic skin, physically based rendering, hyperreal lighting, anime style, cel-shading, comic-book flat colors, low-detail textures, oversharpening, excessive glow effects, floating UI elements, text overlays, logos, watermarks

## Character Reference Sheets

### Theron Ashborne (Protagonist)

**Age and Build:** Early forties (42-43). Lean, scholar's physique shaped by eight years of relentless travel and insufficient rest. Not muscular but wiry with nervous energy and exhaustion. Height: average to slightly above average (5'10"-6'0").

**Facial Structure (High Anatomical Specificity):** Broad, masculine jawline with a subtle cleft; squared chin with rough stubble showing silver-gray mixed with brown. Strong, slightly aquiline nose with a defined bridge; narrow, weathered nasal wings. High cheekbones with subtle hollowing beneath from stress and insufficient food. Deep-set, intelligent eyes (hazel-brown) with dark circles beneath moderately heavy brows. Fine wrinkles at the eyes and mouth corners suggesting a man who once smiled more often; subtle facial asymmetry for realism. Visible pores and minor skin irregularities. Fine-line hatching along the jaw, neck, and cheek planes to indicate shadow, fatigue, and unshaven stubble.

**Hair:** Medium-length brown hair with distinct silver-gray streaks concentrated at the temples and scattered gray strands throughout. Slightly unkempt and travel-worn; natural hairline with mild recession. Individual hair strands visible in silhouette and highlights. Hair texture: straight to slightly wavy.

**Eyes:** Deep-set hazel-brown eyes that shift between calculating intelligence and deep emotional pain. Heavy-lidded with dark circles beneath from exhaustion and grief. Crow's feet at corners. Gaze is direct, focused, often analyzing. When concentrating on magic: intense, almost frighteningly focused.

**Hands:** Scholar's hands with specific details: calloused fingertips from years of precise component work manipulating spell materials. Faint burn scars webbing across palms from magical accidents and experiments. Long, dexterous fingers often in motion—his characteristic gesture is rubbing his thumb across his fingertips (index, middle, ring, pinky) when thinking or calculating. Nails short and practical but often rimmed with component dust.

**Typical Attire:** Dark blue wizard's robes, travel-worn and practical rather than ceremonial. Marked with the inevitable stains of a wizard's work: component dust (various colors), potion splashes, faint scorch marks from magical accidents. Robes are damp and stained during imprisonment scenes, cleaner but still well-used later. Brown leather component pouch at belt (when he has it), bulging with various spell materials. Weathered leather boots suitable for travel.

**Body Language:** Controlled movements with habitual precision. The thumb-rubbing gesture is constant when calculating or thinking. Posture: slightly hunched from years of study and recent exhaustion, but straightens when confronting danger. Hands instinctively go to component pouch when threatened. Carries tension in shoulders and jaw.

**Injury Details (Early Chapters):** Raw rope burns around both wrists, skin reddened and abraded. Minor cuts and grime on hands. Bruising at back of skull (not always visible but implied through hair clumping). Subtle tremor in fingers from cold, exhaustion, and trauma. Gaunt face from days without adequate food.

### Whisper (Familiar - Rat)

**Species and Build:** Large rat, slightly above average size for the species. Dark gray-brown fur with patches of lighter gray at muzzle and chest. Fur appears patchy and damp in dungeon scenes, cleaner and sleeker later.

**Distinctive Features:** Dark, glossy black eyes that reflect light with unusual intelligence—almost human in their expressiveness. Whiskers are long, white, and constantly twitching. Ears slightly tattered from age and long service. Small scars visible on close inspection of muzzle and body.

**Behavioral Tells:** Posture is unnaturally composed for a rat—often sits upright on haunches with almost human poise. Makes direct, sustained eye contact. Movements are purposeful rather than skittish. When speaking through magical connection: whiskers twitch, head tilts with consideration, tail flicks to express emotion (irritation, amusement, concern).

**Personality in Posture:** Ancient weariness combined with dry humor and sharp intelligence. Body language conveys: "I've seen everything, and I'm tired of being underestimated."

### Morganna (The Witch/Antagonist)

**Age and Build:** Appears late twenties to early thirties, but is over eight hundred years old. Tall (5'11"-6'1"), slender build that suggests supernatural preservation rather than natural youth. Uncanny valley effect—beautiful but *wrong* in subtle ways.

**Facial Structure:** Sharp, aristocratic features with high cheekbones and angular jawline. Pale skin with an unnatural, porcelain-like quality—too smooth, too perfect, lacking normal skin texture. Features are symmetrical to an unsettling degree. When calm: hauntingly beautiful. When angry: features sharpen into something predatory and inhuman.

**Hair:** White-silver hair, long and straight, falling past shoulders. In early chapters: elaborately styled with braids and ornaments suggesting ritual significance. During battle: wild, partially burned, streaming around face. Hair has an unnatural sheen, like spider silk or frost.

**Eyes:** Most distinctive feature—black sclera with points of silver-white starlight scattered throughout, like looking into a void filled with distant stars. Pupils are normal-sized black within the starfield. Effect is deeply unsettling. Eyes occasionally glow with purple-black energy during spellcasting.

**Hands:** Long, delicate fingers with sharp, well-maintained nails. Fingers often in motion, tracing sigils in the air or gesturing with theatrical precision. During ritual work: stained with ritual components, blood, silver dust.

**Typical Attire:** Dark robes of deep purple-black with silver embroidery depicting arcane symbols and constellation patterns. Fabric appears to shift and move in ways cloth shouldn't. Multiple rings on fingers, each enchanted. Ritual jewelry: silver circlet with dark gems, amulets layered at chest.

**Body Language:** Theatrical, commanding presence. Moves with predatory grace. Gestures are expansive when confident, sharp and violent when threatened. Carries herself with absolute certainty of her superiority—until that certainty cracks during battle.

**Transformation During Story:** Early scenes: composed, beautiful, terrifying in her control. Battle scenes: disheveled, hair burned and wild, robes scorched, face twisted with rage and desperation. Final scenes: ancient and wounded, mask of beauty slipping to reveal the centuries of pain beneath.
